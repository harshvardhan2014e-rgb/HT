Investment Banking

At Auron India Investment banking is a specialized division of financial institutions that connects investors with entities seeking capital, such as corporations or governments . Unlike commercial banks, investment banks do not take deposits; instead, they focus on advisory services, underwriting, and capital market activities . They play a critical role in the financial system by allocating capital to economically productive uses and facilitating large, complex transactions

Asset Management

Asset management involves the strategic oversight of both financial and physical assets to enhance value over time while minimizing risks and costs. In finance, it focuses on managing investments such as stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and ETFs on behalf of individuals, corporations, or institutions. In business and infrastructure, it extends to physical assets like machinery, buildings, roads, and IT systems, ensuring they are efficiently utilized, maintained, and disposed of when necessary.

Private Equity

Private equity refers to equity investments in companies that are not publicly traded on stock exchanges, often made by specialized investment funds or limited partnerships rather than the general public. PE firms raise capital from institutional investors such as pension funds, hedge funds, endowments, and high-net-worth individuals, pooling it into a fund managed by general partners , while the investors act as limited partners.

Close
Wealth Advisory

A wealth advisor, also known as a wealth management advisor, is a type of financial advisor who primarily works with individuals or families with significant assets, often at least $1 million in liquid assets, though services are not strictly limited to high-net-worth clients. Their main goal is to help clients manage and grow their financial resources while aligning strategies with long-term goals and risk tolerance.
